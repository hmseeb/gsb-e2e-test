Our Origin Story

Born From Obsession

Ember Roasters began in 2018 in the back of a converted garage in Portland, Oregon. Founder Maya Chen had spent a decade as a barista, traveling to coffee farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. She returned with a singular obsession: to roast beans that tasted exactly like the land they came from.

What started as weekend roasting sessions for friends quickly became something more. Word spread. The garage became a pop-up. The pop-up became a shop. Today, Ember Roasters sources directly from six farms across three continents, roasting in small batches to ensure every cup is a revelation.

We believe coffee is more than a morning ritual — it's a connection to soil, sunlight, and the hands that harvested it. Every drink on our menu is a conversation between origin and craft.
